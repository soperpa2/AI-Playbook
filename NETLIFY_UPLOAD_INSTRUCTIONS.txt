AI Playbook Netlify Manual Deploy

Upload the ZIP file named:
AI_Playbook_UPLOAD_THIS_TO_NETLIFY.zip

Do not upload the individual files one at a time. The site needs the folders to stay intact:

- assets
- downloads
- downloads/presentations

How to deploy:
1. Open your Netlify site dashboard.
2. Go to Deploys.
3. Find the manual deploy / drag-and-drop deploy area.
4. Drag AI_Playbook_UPLOAD_THIS_TO_NETLIFY.zip into Netlify.
5. Wait for deploy processing to finish.

The ZIP is built so index.html, app.js, styles.css, assets, and downloads are at the top level of the deployed site.
